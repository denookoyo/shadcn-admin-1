What is FleetOps?

FleetOps is a modern web portal to browse products and services, place orders, schedule appointments (for services), and track your purchases — all in one place.

If you’re just getting started, follow the steps below. You can always come back to this page from Docs.

1) Sign In

  1. Open the site and click Sign In.
  2. Choose Continue with Google and approve the prompt.
We use a secure cookie to keep you signed in. If you switch devices or browsers, you may need to sign in again.

2) Your Profile

  1. Open the user menu (top right) → Profile.
  2. Fill in your Name, Phone, and any optional details.
  3. Click Save.
This information appears to sellers when needed for delivery or scheduling.

3) Browse Products & Services

  • Use the left sidebar to explore categories.
  • Use the search bar at the top to find items quickly.
  • Each item shows a title, price, and seller info. Click an item to see its details and photos.

4) Add to Cart

  • On the item page, choose the quantity and click Add to cart.
  • Open the cart from the top bar to review items.
  • Change quantities or remove items anytime.

5) Checkout

  1. Go to the cart and click Checkout.
  2. Confirm your contact details and delivery preferences.
  3. Place your order. You’ll get an order ID.
Keep your order ID — you can use it to track your order later.

6) Orders & Appointments

Goods (physical products)

  • Paid → Shipped → Completed: we’ll notify you as the order progresses.
  • Mark Received when your package arrives to finalize the order.

Services (appointments)

  • Choose a preferred date/time during checkout, or your seller may propose alternatives.
  • Accept a proposed time from the order page to schedule it.
  • After the service is completed, you can proceed to payment (if applicable).

7) Track an Order

  1. Go to Orders from the sidebar, or open Track Order.
  2. Enter your order ID and email (if using public tracking).
  3. See the current status and any next steps.

8) Reviews & Reports

  • After completion, leave a rating and feedback for the seller.
  • If you had a negative experience, you can file a report from the seller’s page. We take reports seriously.

9) Tips

  • Use the search bar to quickly find items.
  • Switch between light/dark themes using the toggle on the header.
  • Keep your profile up to date to streamline checkout and delivery.

Help & Support

If you need help, first check the order page for notes from the seller. For account issues, ensure you’re signed in with the correct Google account. For anything else, contact support through the portal’s help link.
