Marketplace Rules & Safety
How buyers and sellers interact, how we reduce fraud, and how payments work — in plain language.

How Buying & Selling Works

  1. Browse items, read details, and check seller ratings.
  2. Add to cart and checkout. For services, choose a preferred date/time.
  3. Order placed: sellers get notified. For goods, they ship on time; for services, they confirm or propose new times.
  4. Track progress from your Orders page.
  5. Complete: confirm delivery/service, then leave a review.
Keep all communication and payments inside the portal. It protects your purchase and your privacy.

Ground Rules (Everyone)

  • Be truthful: list accurate details and ask clear questions.
  • Be respectful: no abuse, harassment, or hate speech.
  • Stay on platform: no off‑platform payments or contact sharing.
  • No prohibited items/services: follow local laws and our policies.
  • Protect privacy: share only what’s needed to fulfill the order.

For Sellers

  • Provide clear photos, honest descriptions, and real availability.
  • Ship within the stated timeframe; add tracking where possible.
  • For services: confirm or propose a new time promptly.
  • Respond to messages within 24 hours.
  • Keep all payments inside the portal. No surcharges or side deals.

For Buyers

  • Use your real name and a valid address or service location.
  • Be reachable for delivery or appointment times.
  • Pay through the portal only; don’t share card or bank details in chat.
  • Mark orders Received when delivered; don’t mark if there’s an issue.
  • Leave fair reviews to help others.

Reducing Fraud

  • Verified sellers: we may request ID/ABN and business details.
  • Escrow holds: we hold funds until shipment or service completion.
  • Risk checks: extra checks on unusual or high‑value orders.
  • 3‑D Secure for cards, and delivery to verified addresses.
  • Report & block: flag suspicious behavior from any page.

Disputes & Refunds

  1. Try to resolve in the order messages (stay courteous and factual).
  2. Open a dispute if unresolved. Add photos, tracking, or chat screenshots.
  3. We review the case using evidence from both sides.
  4. Outcome: refund, replacement/re‑work, partial credit, or close with notes.

Time limits apply. If you paid outside the portal, we can’t help recover funds.

How Payments Work

  • Central payments through our provider (e.g., cards, Apple/Google Pay, PayPal).
  • Funds are held until the seller ships or the service is completed.
  • Payouts go to sellers after delivery/confirmation, minus fees.
  • Chargebacks & disputes are handled with the payment provider.
Paying within the portal keeps you covered by our Buyer Protection Program.

Your Choices & Why

  • Buyers can choose a payment method (card, wallet) inside the portal.
  • We don’t allow paying sellers directly (bank transfers, crypto, cash) because it removes protection and increases fraud risk.
  • Some large orders may require extra checks or a deposit.

If a seller asks to pay outside the portal, decline and report it.

Why Centralised Payments?

  • Protection: refunds, chargebacks, and escrow are available.
  • Consistency: same experience for every order and method.
  • Compliance: KYC/AML and tax reporting are handled properly.
  • Fewer scams: no off‑platform accounts or fake links.

Letting each seller collect money directly leads to inconsistent refunds, higher fraud, and legal obligations for both buyers and sellers. We avoid that to keep everyone safer.

Privacy & Safety

  • Share only what’s needed to deliver the order or complete the service.
  • Never share passwords or full card numbers in chat.
  • We keep audit logs for serious issues and law‑abiding investigations.
