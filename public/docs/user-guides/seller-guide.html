Seller Guide
Everything you need to start selling on Hedgetech Marketplace.

Before You Start

  • Sign in with Google and complete your Profile (name, image, contact).
  • For business sellers, have your ABN/ID and bank payout details ready (if required).
  • Decide if you are selling Goods (physical items) or Services (appointments/jobs) — or both.

Add a Product (Goods)

  1. Go to Products → Add Product.
  2. Fill in Title, Price, and set Type to Goods.
  3. Upload images and write a clear description (benefits, size/materials, what’s included).
  4. Choose a Category. Save.
Use high‑quality photos on a clean background. Include measurements and care instructions if relevant.

Add a Service

  1. Go to Products → Add Product.
  2. Set Type to Service. Describe what you do and typical duration.
  3. Add price (fixed or starting price) and any location/coverage notes.
  4. Upload images of past work or representative visuals. Save.
Mention availability windows (e.g., weekdays 9–5), required access, and what the customer should prepare.

Manage Your Listings

  • Edit a listing anytime from Products → select an item → Edit.
  • Keep prices and stock current. Pause a listing if out of stock.
  • Update descriptions if you receive repeated questions.

Accept & Fulfil Orders (Goods)

  1. Open Orders → Seller Orders.
  2. New orders appear as Pending. Review details and confirm you can fulfil.
  3. Pack and Ship by the promised timeframe (add tracking in the order notes if available).
  4. Mark Shipped in the order when dispatched.
  5. After the buyer confirms receipt, the order moves to Completed and payout is scheduled.
If you cannot fulfil an item, message the buyer promptly with options (refund, alternative, or delay) and use the dispute tools if needed.

Accept & Schedule Orders (Services)

  1. Open Orders → Seller Orders.
  2. For service items, confirm the buyer’s requested time or click Propose Alternates to offer 1–3 new times.
  3. When the buyer accepts a time, the appointment becomes Scheduled.
  4. Complete the job and update the order to Completed.
Be specific when proposing times (date, time window). If weather or access is a factor, note it in the message.

Rescheduling (Reappointment)

  • From the order page, choose Reject/Propose and enter alternate date/time options.
  • If the buyer proposes a new time, review and Accept if you can make it.
  • Keep all scheduling messages inside the order to maintain a clear record.

Messaging & Customer Care

  • Respond within 24 hours. Be polite and solution‑oriented.
  • Share only necessary details. No personal payment links or contact info.
  • Use photos or short videos to clarify issues when helpful.

Payments & Payouts

  • Buyers pay inside the portal via secure providers (cards, wallets).
  • Funds are held until shipment (goods) or completion (services), then paid out to you minus fees.
  • Chargebacks/disputes are handled through the provider — keep evidence.

Never ask for off‑platform payment; it voids protection and may lead to account actions.

Cancellations, Returns, and Disputes

  • Cancellations: if you cannot fulfil, cancel promptly and explain why.
  • Returns (goods): share a return address and conditions (unused, original packaging).
  • Disputes: submit clear evidence (photos, tracking, chat) for a fair outcome.

Best Practices

  • Maintain accurate stock and availability.
  • Use templates for common replies (shipping times, preparation steps).
  • Ask satisfied buyers to leave a review — it builds trust.

Need Help?

Check the Marketplace Rules & Safety for required conduct and protections. For account issues, use the portal’s Help link.
